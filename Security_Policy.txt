Document Title: IT Security Policy
Organization: A2Z
Version: 1.0
Effective From: [Insert Date]
Owner: IT Security Department

1. Purpose
To ensure confidentiality, integrity, and availability of all company IT systems and data.

2. Scope
Applies to all employees, contractors, and third-party vendors using company resources.

3. Password Policy
Minimum 8 characters with uppercase, lowercase, number, and symbol.

Passwords must be changed every 90 days.

Password sharing is strictly prohibited.

4. Access Control
Role-based access will be granted by the IT team.

All access changes must be approved and logged.

5. Antivirus & Updates
All devices must have up-to-date antivirus software.

Operating systems and software should have latest security patches applied.

6. Remote Access
VPN must be used for remote connections.

Public Wi-Fi must be avoided unless connected via VPN.

7. Incident Reporting
Any suspected breach must be reported within 30 minutes to security@[yourdomain].com

Users must not attempt to resolve incidents on their own.

8. Physical Security
Server rooms are restricted to authorized personnel only.

All visitors must sign in and be accompanied by a staff member.

9. Data Protection
All sensitive data must be encrypted in transit and at rest.

Data sharing with third parties must comply with legal and internal guidelines.

10. Enforcement
Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary actions.

Severe cases may lead to termination or legal proceedings.
